AI Post & Social Media Portfolio

As an AI Developer Advocate Intern at AI Post (AI郵報), a leading AI-focused media outlet in Taiwan with over 100,000 subscribers, I had the opportunity to research, write, and publish a wide range of content. My work covered in-depth technical tutorials, AI industry news analysis, and educational social media content. This portfolio showcases my key contributions, demonstrating my ability to communicate complex AI concepts to diverse audiences and my passion for the field.

Vibe Coding: AI-Powered Development & Automation

This series focuses on practical tutorials for developers and tech enthusiasts, exploring how to leverage the latest AI tools to accelerate and simplify development workflows. I authored numerous articles in this popular series.

    • Published: Oct 30, 2025

  • Getting Started with Claude Skills: Train Your AI Assistant

    • Published: Oct 23, 2025
    • Introduced the new “Skills” feature in Claude, showing readers how to teach the AI to adopt their specific workflows for tasks ranging from brand design to programming.

    • Published: Oct 16, 2025
    • A practical guide on using Claude for code generation, shadcn-ui for styling, and Vercel for one-click deployment to quickly build and launch a website from scratch.

  • 18 Lessons from 2500+ AI Conversations: A Developer’s Guide

    • Published: Oct 9, 2025
    • Shared practical, hard-won insights from over 2,500 interactions with AI coding assistants like Cursor, providing a playbook for avoiding common pitfalls in AI-assisted development.

  • Mastering AI Prompts with the CLEAR Framework

    • Published: Oct 2, 2025
    • This article distills official prompt engineering guidelines into a practical framework (CLEAR) and four levels of techniques to help users create high-quality, efficient workflows with LLMs.

Her Path in Tech: Empowering Women in AI

Recognizing a critical gap in support for female innovators, I founded Her Path in Tech, a community and event series dedicated to empowering women in the technology industry. This initiative was born from the observation that while many women attended AI workshops, they often hesitated to share their work or pursue entrepreneurial ventures.

As the founder, I managed all aspects of the series, including designing monthly event topics, recruiting senior female speakers from major tech companies, and marketing and hosting the sessions to ensure a welcoming and supportive atmosphere.

The impact of this initiative was significant, attracting over 500 cumulative participants and receiving outstanding feedback with an average Willingness to Recommend score of 9.25/10 and a Content Usefulness score of 9.04/10. Most importantly, it cultivated a thriving community where participants collaborate on projects, share job opportunities, and support each other’s career growth.

Her Path in Tech demonstrated that with a dedicated and inclusive support network, women are not just participants but active leaders and innovators in the AI field. This project represents my commitment to building inclusive and scalable platforms that democratize access to AI and entrepreneurship.
